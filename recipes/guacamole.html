Description

Fresh, zesty guacamole in 5 minutes!

ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 small tomato (diced)
  • Salt & cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl
  2. Add lime juice, onion, tomato, and salt
  3. Mix gently, top with cilantro. Serve with chips!
