Description

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for breakfast. Ready in 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt)
  2. Add wet ingredients (milk, egg, butter) and whisk until smooth
  3. Heat a pan on medium, pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake
  4. Flip when bubbles form, cook until golden. Serve with syrup!
