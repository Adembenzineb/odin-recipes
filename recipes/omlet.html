Omlette

Description

A quick and fluffy omelette made with eggs, filled with your favorite ingredients like
cheese or veggies. Ready in 5 minutes!

Ingredience

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 tsp butter/oil
  • Filling (cheese, ham, veggies â€“ optional)

Steps

  1. Beat 2 eggs + salt + pepper
  2. Melt butter in pan, pour eggs.
  3. Add filling (cheese/veggies), fold, cook 1 min.
