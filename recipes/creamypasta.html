Description

Rich, creamy pasta in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as per package instructions
  2. Melt butter in a pan, add cream, and simmer
  3. Stir in Parmesan until smooth
  4. Toss with pasta, season, and serve!
